Example Description

This example describes how to use pwm

Requirement Components:
    1~4 LED

Connect LED to below PWM pins and ground, then the LED would gradually become brighter and then darker with different speed.
HS
 - connect a LED to PG_4 and ground
 - connect a LED to PG_5 and ground
 - connect a LED to PG_8 and ground
 - connect a LED to PG_9 and ground

LS
 - connect a LED to PA_2 and ground
 - connect a LED to PA_3 and ground
 - connect a LED to PA_4 and ground
 - connect a LED to PA_5 and ground
